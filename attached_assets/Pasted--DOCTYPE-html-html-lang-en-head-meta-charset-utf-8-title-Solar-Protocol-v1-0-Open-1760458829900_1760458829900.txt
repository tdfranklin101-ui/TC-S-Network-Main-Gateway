  <h1>Solar Protocol v1.0 — Open Energy Equivalence Standard</h1>
  <p><strong>1 Solar = 4,913 kWh</strong>. The Solar Protocol defines a universal reference for energy-based valuation across AI, compute, and digital assets. It embeds human-rights alignment and ecological accountability directly into machine-readable metadata.</p>

  <h2>Purpose</h2>
  <p>To create a transparent, auditable, and ethical baseline for energy accounting in the age of artificial intelligence and distributed compute. Each Solar represents a day’s worth of clean, renewable human energy income.</p>
