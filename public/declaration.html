The Current-See Declaration of Solar Value

We, The Current-See PBC, Inc., do hereby recognize that Earth receives approximately 174 petawatts of solar energy per day—a resource both abundant and renewable, yet historically unmonetized for the benefit of the global public.

By activating the Solar Clock at 00:00 GMT on April 7, 2025, we declare the right to tokenize 1% of this daily solar input—equivalent to 1.74 petawatts—as a publicly recorded, freely distributable energy-denominated asset, known as the Solar.

This declaration marks the moment of origination—the first official recording of value derived from a natural, measurable planetary resource. Ownership of each daily issuance is distributed not by conquest or capital, but by participation and registration in the Current-See Network.

The Solar is not extracted—it is recognized, quantified, and allocated. It is value without depletion.

This system shall remain open, transparent, and guided by the principles of equity, energy stewardship, and planetary citizenship.
