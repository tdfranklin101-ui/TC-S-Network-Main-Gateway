Account Information

Email Verification Coming Soon

We're implementing a secure email verification system to protect your SOLAR distribution account. Soon, you'll be able to:

  • Securely verify your identity via email
  • Set up password protection for your account
  • Manage your SOLAR distribution settings
  • Access advanced features of the Current-See platform

This enhancement supports our commitment to a transparent and secure global economic system.

Already Registered?

If you've already joined The Current-See using our sign-up form, your account is safely stored in our system. When our email verification system launches, you'll receive an email with instructions to complete your account setup.

In the meantime, you can track your SOLAR distributions in the upcoming account dashboard feature.
