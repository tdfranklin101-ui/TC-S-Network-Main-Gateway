Founder's Note

By Terry D. Franklin

The Current-See is a vision for a more equitable global economic system—one that is backed by the abundance of solar energy rather than the scarcity of traditional resources. This concept began with a simple question: What if we could create a currency that grew with the sun?

Our planet receives more energy from the sun in a single hour than humanity uses in an entire year. This incredible abundance has shaped my belief that we can create an economic system based on this renewable, sustainable resource—turning sunlight into a form of universal basic income for all people.

"One Solar. One Earth. Daily."

The Current-See model is elegantly simple: 1% of Earth's daily solar input, converted to kilowatt-hours and distributed equally among the world's population. This creates a baseline of economic value that is:

• Scientifically measured
• Universally distributed
• Environmentally aligned
• Continuously renewed

This prototype represents the first step toward that vision. By joining our prototype community, you'll begin receiving daily solar distributions—digitally tracked Solars that represent your personal share of Earth's solar abundance.

I invite you to imagine a world where everyone holds stake in the sun's energy—where economic power grows with renewable abundance rather than extractive scarcity. Together, we're creating a new kind of current-see: the flow of solar value to all people, every day.

Thank you for being part of this journey.

With gratitude, Terry D. Franklin April 10, 2025
