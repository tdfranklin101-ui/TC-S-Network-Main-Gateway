Founder's Note

By Terry D. Franklin

Before Ray Kurzweil there was Buckminster Fuller. In the 1920's Fuller determined to see what one man could do to assist humanity's survival. Being ahead of his time has found that human progress has found and used his work to further humanity.

A book called Critical Path Fuller wrote as a sign post. Caring for the earth like a space ship requires space age planning (NASA invented CPM-scheduling to get to the moon). Fuller had an idea that the citizens own the space ship and need to steward sustainably to fulfill humanities mission to keep boldly going on. Fuller envisioned a solar backed currency that was a birthright of every citizen on the planet. Monetizing and distributing an amount equivalent to the daily interest on $1 billion dollars based on a sustainable energy standard (kWh from the sun) to every individual is on the critical path.

"We are living in the design and engineering revolution/evolution that Fuller predicted."

As an individual citizen of Space Ship Earth I am using the current technology available to me now to bring Buckminster Fuller's dream of a solar backed currency to reality.

I think that is what Bucky would do today.

Terry D. Franklin The Current-See, PBC, Inc. TC-S Network April 9, 2025
