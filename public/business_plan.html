Business Plan: The Current See PBC, Inc.

April 16, 2025

Executive Summary

The Current See PBC, Inc. is a Public Benefit Corporation committed to advancing sustainable economic systems through innovative financial technologies. Our flagship initiative, the Token Currency System (TC-S) Network, empowers communities, organizations, and events to establish their own internal economies via customizable private currency networks. By integrating gamified token systems and AI-driven wallets, we enhance financial inclusion, promote economic resilience, and support sustainable development.

Mission Statement

To develop and deploy scalable, private currency networks that enable communities and organizations to establish autonomous, purpose-driven economies, thereby advancing financial empowerment and social equity.

Public Benefit Objectives

  • Financial Inclusion: Provide tools for underserved communities to participate in digital economies.
  • Economic Resilience: Enable organizations to create internal economies that can withstand external economic fluctuations.
  • Sustainable Development: Support eco-friendly initiatives by facilitating transactions that promote environmental responsibility.

Products and Services

1. TC-S Network Platform

A modular platform allowing clients to:

  • Design Custom Tokens: Create branded digital currencies tailored to specific events or communities.
  • Implement AI Wallets: Deploy user-friendly wallets that facilitate seamless transactions via QR codes.
  • Manage Private Ledgers: Maintain secure, transparent records of all transactions within the network.
  • Incorporate Gamification Elements: Integrate reward systems to boost engagement and participation.

2. Consulting and Implementation Services

  • Needs Assessment: Evaluate client requirements to tailor the TC-S Network accordingly.
  • Deployment Support: Assist in the technical setup and launch of the private currency network.
  • Training Programs: Educate stakeholders on effectively utilizing the system.

Market Analysis

Target Markets

  • Event Organizers: Festivals, conferences, and exhibitions seeking efficient transaction systems.
  • Hospitality Industry: Resorts and hotels aiming to enhance guest experiences through cashless transactions.
  • Community Organizations: Groups interested in fostering local economies and incentivizing participation.
  • Government Agencies: Entities exploring basic income programs or localized economic initiatives.
  • Corporate Employers: Companies seeking innovative methods to engage and reward employees.

Competitive Advantage

While other platforms offer digital payment solutions, our TC-S Network stands out by:

  • Customization: Allowing clients to design tokens that reflect their unique identity and objectives.
  • Gamification: Incorporating reward systems to boost engagement and participation.
  • Scalability: Supporting a range of applications, from small community events to large-scale governmental programs.

Monetization Strategy

The TC-S Network supports two primary monetization models to cater to varying organizational needs:

1. Corporate Model

In the Corporate Model, The Current See PBC, Inc. licenses the TC-S Network technology to clients. This licensing provides:

  • Branded Currency: Clients receive a customized digital currency that aligns with their brand identity.
  • Branded AI Wallet Apps: Deployment of AI-driven wallet applications tailored to the client's branding, facilitating seamless transactions within their private network.
  • Private Network Infrastructure: A secure and scalable infrastructure to support the client's internal economy.

Licensing is based on a user group value, allowing flexibility to accommodate organizations, venues, and events of various sizes. The license includes per-use monetization metrics, enabling The Current See PBC, Inc. to generate revenue based on network activity and usage levels.

2. Public Benefit Model

In the Public Benefit Model, The Current See PBC, Inc. partners and licenses to governmental and non-profit organizations to deploy the TC-S Network as a tool for social impact. This model includes:

  • Token Allocation: Distribution of tokens to participants as a form of support or incentive, without direct purchase.
  • Community Engagement: Encouraging inclusivity and participation, particularly benefiting those who may face financial barriers.
  • Funding Sources: Seeking grants, donations, or public funding to support the distribution of tokens and maintenance of the network.
  • Impact Reporting: Providing transparent reports on the social and economic impact of the token distribution to stakeholders and funders.

Operational Plan

Phase 1: Development

  • Platform Enhancement: Refine the TC-S Network based on pilot feedback.
  • AI Wallet Optimization: Ensure seamless user experience across devices.

Phase 2: Pilot Programs

  • Community Trials: Implement the system in select communities to assess impact and gather data.
  • Event Partnerships: Collaborate with event organizers to showcase the platform's capabilities.

Phase 3: Expansion

  • Market Outreach: Engage with potential clients across target markets.
  • Continuous Improvement: Iterate on the platform based on user feedback and technological advancements.

Private Currency Networks for Employee Engagement

Overview

Beyond external applications, the TC-S Network can be utilized internally by companies to enhance employee engagement and satisfaction. By implementing private currency systems within the workplace, employers can create dynamic reward and recognition programs that foster a positive organizational culture.

Applications

  • Recognition Programs: Employees earn tokens for exemplary performance, teamwork, or innovation, which can be redeemed for various rewards.
  • Wellness Incentives: Encourage healthy behaviors by awarding tokens for participation in wellness activities.
  • Professional Development: Incentivize skill development and continuous learning through token-based rewards.
  • Peer-to-Peer Recognition: Empower employees to acknowledge each other's contributions, strengthening team cohesion.

Benefits

  • Enhanced Motivation: Gamified rewards systems can boost morale and productivity.
  • Customization: Tailor the currency system to align with company values and objectives.
  • Data Insights: Track engagement metrics to inform HR strategies and initiatives.

Case Example: Zappos

Zappos has successfully implemented internal reward systems, such as "Zollars," to recognize and incentivize employee performance. Employees can give each other "Zollars" when caught doing something that WOWs them. These colorful play-money bills have no value outside of the Zappos ecosystem but can be redeemed for various rewards, ranging from practical items to charitable initiatives. This approach has led to increased engagement and a positive workplace culture.

Potential Use Case: Burning Man

Overview

Burning Man, held annually in Nevada's Black Rock Desert, exemplifies a large-scale, temporary community operating on principles of radical self-expression, self-reliance, and gifting. With over 70,000 participants, the event fosters a unique environment where traditional commerce is absent, and participants are encouraged to share resources freely.

Challenges in the Current Model

Despite its emphasis on a gift economy, the logistical demands of sustaining such a large population in a harsh environment present challenges:

  • Resource Distribution: Ensuring equitable access to food, water, and supplies without traditional transactional systems.
  • Participant Engagement: Encouraging active participation in communal responsibilities and contributions.
  • Scalability: Maintaining the principles of the event as attendance grows and diversifies.

TC-S Network Implementation

The Token Currency System (TC-S) Network offers a solution by introducing a customizable, private token exchange system that aligns with Burning Man's ethos:

  • Token-Based Resource Allocation: Participants receive tokens that can be exchanged for goods and services, facilitating resource distribution while preserving the non-commercial spirit.
  • Incentivized Participation: Tokens can be earned through contributions to the community, such as volunteering or providing services, promoting active engagement.
  • Customizable Ecosystem: The token system can be tailored to reflect the unique culture and values of Burning Man, ensuring alignment with its core principles.

Dual Monetization Models Supported by TC-S

The TC-S Network accommodates two distinct models to support such events:

Corporate Model:

  • Token Purchase: Participants or organizations purchase tokens to access goods and services within the private network.
  • Revenue Generation: Funds generated from token sales can support event infrastructure, logistics, and other operational costs.

Public Benefit Model:

  • Token Allocation: Tokens are distributed to participants as a form of support or incentive, without direct purchase.
  • Community Engagement: Encourages inclusivity and participation, particularly benefiting those who may face financial barriers.

By integrating the TC-S Network into events like Burning Man, organizers can enhance resource management, participant engagement, and scalability, all while upholding the foundational values of the community.
