How It Works

When you join, you'll be assigned a unique solar account. Every day, your account will receive 1 SOLAR unit, representing your share of the sun's energy that reaches Earth.

The Current-See system tracks the cumulative energy production since April 7, 2025, and distributes it equitably to all members.
