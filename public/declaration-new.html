The Solar Declaration

A Foundation for a Solar-Backed Economy

We, the founders and members of The Current-See, declare that the Sun's energy is the rightful inheritance of all people on Earth. With this declaration, we establish a new economic system based on the fundamental truth that the Sun's daily energy input to our planet represents a renewable, sustainable, and abundant resource that belongs to all humanity.

"The Sun shines equally on all people. Its energy should flow to all people equally."

We hold these principles to be self-evident:

1. Solar Energy as a Universal Right: The Sun's energy is not owned by any nation, corporation, or individual. It is a universal resource that should benefit all people equally.

2. Equal Solar Distributions: Every person on Earth is entitled to an equal share of the monetized value of the Sun's daily energy input to our planet.

3. Scientific Measurement: Solar distributions shall be based on rigorous scientific measurements of solar energy reaching Earth, initially calculated as 1% of the total daily input converted to kilowatt-hours.

4. Daily Renewal: Solar distributions shall occur daily, reflecting the continuous nature of solar energy input to our planet.

5. Transparent Accounting: All Solar accounts shall be maintained in a transparent, verifiable manner to ensure trust and accountability.

6. Environmental Alignment: The solar-backed economic system shall fundamentally align human economic activity with environmental sustainability.

7. Economic Inclusion: The solar-backed economic system shall provide basic economic participation to all people, regardless of nationality, gender, race, or socioeconomic status.

8. Technological Implementation: We will utilize appropriate technologies to ensure efficient, secure, and accessible distribution of Solar currency to all participants.

9. Global Scale: While beginning as a prototype, the solar economic system is designed to scale to include all people on Earth.

10. Peaceful Transition: The implementation of this system shall occur through peaceful, voluntary participation and gradual adoption, respecting existing economic structures while offering a transformative alternative.

In signing this declaration through our participation in The Current-See prototype, we commit to building and supporting an economic system where solar energy becomes the foundation of universal prosperity and environmental sustainability.

One Solar. One Earth. Daily.
