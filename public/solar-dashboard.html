Educational & Research Disclaimer (GENIUS Act‚ÄìAligned)

The Solar Reserve Tracker is an open, educational data aggregator. It compiles and normalizes renewable-energy metrics from verified sources to illustrate global kWh flows and their Solar-equivalent representation (1 Solar = 4,913 kWh).

  • Purpose: research, education, transparency, and system testing under GENIUS Act principles.
  • No legal tender: Solar is not fiat, security, REC, or energy credit in any jurisdiction.
  • Limited use: Solar functions only inside the TC-S Network Marketplace as a peer-to-peer unit for digital artifacts where every member is both buyer and seller.
  • No implied valuation: conversions, charts, and deltas are illustrative and non-financial.
Important: The tracker does not mint, issue, or promise redemption of any monetary instrument. It reports auditable energy-based metrics for educational demonstration and governance research.
