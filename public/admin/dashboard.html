About the Solar Clock

The Solar Clock represents the continuous generation of solar energy and its equivalent monetary value. The Current-See system uses this model to back its economic framework.

Current Configuration:

  • • 1 Solar = 17.7 million kWh
  • • Value per Solar = $136,000 USD
  • • Generation Period = 24 hours (daily)

This value represents the approximate energy output of sunlight hitting Earth's surface over a 24-hour period and its equivalent value at current electricity rates.

Solar Clock Settings

These settings determine how the Solar Clock calculates and displays values.
